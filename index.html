Our commitment to your privacy

Your privacy is not an afterthought at YaarWin — it is one of the foundations on which the platform is built. This policy explains, in plain language, what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the rights you have over it. We have deliberately written this document to be readable rather than legalistic, because we believe a privacy policy that nobody reads is barely a policy at all. If anything in this document is unclear, you can always reach our team through the support tab inside your account.

What information we collect

When you create a YaarWin account we collect your registered mobile number and a hashed version of your password. Your password is never stored in plain text and cannot be retrieved by any member of our team — it can only be reset, never read. As you use the platform we record wallet transactions, prediction history and giftcode redemptions, all of which are necessary to operate your account. For security and fraud-prevention purposes we also log technical metadata such as device type, app version, IP address at login, and approximate access location. When you go to withdraw, we collect identity verification information as required by financial regulations. We never collect more than what is needed for the legitimate operation of your account.

How we use your information

Your information is used to operate your account, process your wallet transactions, deliver giftcodes and notifications you have opted into, secure the platform against fraud, comply with applicable laws, and improve the product over time through aggregated, non-identifying usage analytics. We do not use your information to build advertising profiles, to target you with third-party marketing, or for any purpose unrelated to the operation of your YaarWin account.

How we share your information

We share information only with carefully vetted service providers who help operate the platform — payment processors for UPI and IMPS payouts, cloud infrastructure providers for hosting and backups, security services for fraud detection, and customer support tooling. Each provider is bound by contractual confidentiality and data-processing terms equivalent to or stricter than those described in this policy. We may also disclose information when required by valid legal process, when necessary to protect the rights or safety of users, or in connection with a corporate transaction such as a merger or acquisition, in which case users will be notified through the app.

How we protect your information

Security at YaarWin is designed in layers. All network traffic uses HTTPS with strong ciphers. Passwords are hashed with industry-standard algorithms. Wallets are stored in segregated databases with continuous encrypted backups. Internal access to player data is restricted, logged and reviewed. Two-factor authentication is available to every account and strongly encouraged. Adaptive risk scoring flags unusual access patterns and may trigger additional verification before sensitive actions are allowed. No security system is unbreakable, but ours is engineered to make a successful attack expensive enough to deter all but the most determined and well-resourced adversaries.

Your rights over your information

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate information, request export of your data in a portable format, and request deletion of your account and associated data. You also have the right to withdraw consent for any optional data processing — for example, you can disable marketing notifications at any time from inside your account settings. To exercise any of these rights, open the support tab in your YaarWin account and submit a data request. We aim to respond to all requests within a few business days, subject to identity verification.

Data retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. After an account is closed or deleted, most personal information is removed within a reasonable period, while a minimal set of records is retained for the period required by financial, anti-fraud and anti-money-laundering regulations. Backups containing data are rotated on a regular schedule and overwritten as part of normal infrastructure operations.

Cookies and similar technologies

The YaarWin website uses a small number of cookies and equivalent local storage entries to maintain your login session, remember your preferences, and gather aggregated, non-identifying analytics that help us improve the platform. Essential cookies cannot be disabled because they are required for the site to function. Analytics cookies can be disabled at the browser level if you prefer. We do not use third-party advertising cookies of any kind.

Children's privacy

YaarWin is intended exclusively for adults of legal age in the jurisdiction where they reside. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware that an account has been created by a minor, we will close the account and delete the associated data promptly.

International data transfers

Some of our service providers operate infrastructure in multiple geographic regions. Where personal information is transferred internationally, the receiving provider is contractually bound to maintain protection equivalent to that described in this policy. By using YaarWin you consent to such transfers as necessary for the operation of the platform.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the platform, in our practices, or in applicable law. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notification and a banner on the website ahead of the effective date. Continued use of YaarWin after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. The most current version of this policy is always the one published on this page.

Contact us about privacy

If you have any questions about this policy, the data we hold about you, or how to exercise your rights, please reach our team through the support tab inside your YaarWin account. We treat every privacy request seriously and will respond as quickly as possible. Your trust is the foundation of this platform, and protecting your data is how we earn that trust every single day.
